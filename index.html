Research

Published work.

1st Author ICSSAS 2025 · IEEE Xplore

Mentor Connect: An AI-Driven Real-Time Mentorship Platform

An AI-powered mentorship platform closing the accessibility gap between mentors and mentees through real-time, role-based communication. Features a Graph Neural Network-based recommendation engine for personalized mentor matching and live video mentoring via Jitsi Meet. Experiments showed accurate mentor allocations, low-latency video interactions, and effective handling of concurrent user activity — enabling scalable, personalized, and accessible mentorship, especially in digitally disadvantaged settings.

Shreyus Rajiv, R. Mukherjee, V. Angayarkanni, J. Kalaivani, P. Rama View on IEEE Xplore ↗
